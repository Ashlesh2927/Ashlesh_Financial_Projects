GST COMPLIANCE & CALCULATION GUIDE
===================================

What is GST?
Goods and Services Tax - Indirect tax on products/services

Types of GST:
1. CGST (Central) - 9%
2. SGST (State) - 9%
3. IGST (Inter-state) - 18%

Total for same state: CGST + SGST = 18%
Different state: IGST = 18%

---

EXAMPLE 1: INTRA-STATE (Same State) Transaction
Product: Laptop
Price (Without GST): ₹50,000

Calculation:
CGST (9%) = 50,000 × 9/100 = ₹4,500
SGST (9%) = 50,000 × 9/100 = ₹4,500
Total GST = ₹9,000
Final Price = ₹59,000

---

EXAMPLE 2: INTER-STATE Transaction
Product: Mobile Phone
Price (Without GST): ₹30,000

Calculation:
IGST (18%) = 30,000 × 18/100 = ₹5,400
Final Price = ₹35,400

---

EXAMPLE 3: Multiple Products (Same Invoice)

Item          Price    GST%   GST Amt   Total
Laptop        50,000   18%    9,000     59,000
Mobile        30,000   18%    5,400     35,400
Headphones    5,000    18%    900       5,900
─────────────────────────────────────────────
TOTAL         85,000          15,300    100,300

---

GST Registration Requirement:
- Turnover > ₹40 lakhs = Compulsory
- Turnover < ₹40 lakhs = Optional
