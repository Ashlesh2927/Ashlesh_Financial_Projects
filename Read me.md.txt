## Project Overview
This folder contains 2 practical Excel spreadsheets demonstrating 
financial calculations and expense management.

## What's Included?

### 1. GST Calculator
**Purpose:** Calculate GST amount and total price for products

**How it works:**
- Enter product name in A3
- Enter price (without GST) in B4
- GST rate automatically calculated (18%)
- Formula: GST Amount = Price × (Rate/100)
- Total Price = Price + GST Amount

**Real-world use:**
This helps accountants and business owners quickly calculate 
product prices with GST during invoicing.

**Example:**
Laptop Price: ₹50,000
GST Rate: 18%
GST Amount: ₹9,000 (calculated by formula)
Final Price: ₹59,000

---

### 2. Expense Tracker
**Purpose:** Track monthly expenses and calculate totals

**How it works:**
- Enter date, category, and amount
- Running total automatically calculates
- Total sum at bottom
- Average monthly spending shown

**Formula Used:**
- Running Total: =SUM($C$2:C2) [This is absolute reference]
- Total: =SUM(C2:C5)
- Average: =AVERAGE(C2:C5)

**Real-world use:**
Personal finance management, tracking household expenses, 
budget planning.
